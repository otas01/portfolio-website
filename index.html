About me

My name is Otas. I'm a web developer based in Londonderry, Northern Ireland, United Kingdom.

The journey to becoming a web developer began as a hobby and something to do alongside practicing as a medical doctor and I somehow found myself here. Web develepment has grown on me and is something I genuinely enjoy just as much as being a doctor.

The feeling you get when you create something out of nothing is unparalleled and that's why I specialize in creating beautiful and responsive websites tailored meticulously to individual requests.

I'm married to an incredible woman and when I'm not behind my computer screen or saving lives, I am hanging out with friends or playing football.

Think we can create something together? Contact me at your earliest convenience and let's create greatness.
